2. What you'll get

The level of your disability will affect the amount of benefit you may get. This will be assessed by a ‘medical advisor’ on a scale of 1 to 100%

Normally you must be assessed as 14% disabled or more to get the benefit

All amounts are guides only

Assessed level of disablement Weekly amonut

How you’re paid

Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit is usually paid weekly in advance, 4 weekly in arrears or every 13 weeks in arrears. It will normally be paid into an account, eg a bank account, building society or Credit Union.
